About

At the heart of my diverse skill set lies my passion for fashion. As a seasoned fashion model, I've graced the runways of prestigious events like Hub of Africa, The Bag Show , and exclusive showcases for renowned designers like Genet, the creative mind behind Paradise. From Ethiopia Tamrt to Mintex, I've lent my distinctive style and poise to numerous fashion shows, leaving an indelible mark on the industry. As a Computer Science graduate with a passion for pushing boundaries, I bring a unique blend of technical expertise and artistic flair to everything I do.

But my talents extend far beyond the catwalk. As an Event Coordinator, I orchestrate seamless experiences for guests at events such as Hub of Africa and Design Week Addis Ababa, ensuring every detail is perfect and every moment unforgettable. Whether it's managing guest lists, coordinating logistics, or overseeing projects from conception to execution, I thrive on the challenge of bringing visions to life.

In the fast-paced world of entertainment, I've proven my versatility time and time again. From serving as a backstage manager for cutting-edge Tecno product launches to taking on the role of project manager for the acclaimed TV show AlBash, I bring a unique blend of creativity and precision to every project.
